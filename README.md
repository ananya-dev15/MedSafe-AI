# 🩺 MedSafe AI

MedSafe AI is an intelligent AI-powered healthcare assistance platform designed to improve **medicine safety awareness, prescription understanding, and early health risk identification**.

The system combines **Artificial Intelligence, Optical Character Recognition (OCR), fuzzy matching algorithms, and curated medical datasets** to help users safely understand medication usage and basic health concerns.

MedSafe AI aims to support **patients, caregivers, students, and healthcare learners** by providing simplified explanations and risk awareness while emphasizing that the platform is **for educational purposes only and does not replace professional medical advice.**

---

# 🚀 Features

### 💊 Medicine Interaction Checker
Detects potential **drug–drug interactions** between medicines entered by the user and provides AI-generated explanations of possible health risks.

### 📄 Prescription OCR Reader
Users can upload a **prescription image**, and the system extracts medicine names using **EasyOCR** and identifies possible medications.

### 🤒 Symptom Guidance System
Users can enter symptoms to receive **basic health guidance and AI-based explanations** about possible causes.

### 📋 Side Effect Monitor
Allows users to log **post-medication experiences and side effects** for tracking health reactions.

### 🚨 Emergency Risk Predictor
Analyzes symptoms and provides **risk-level alerts (Low, Medium, High)** along with AI explanations for possible medical emergencies.

---

# 🧠 AI Capabilities

MedSafe AI integrates **Generative AI** to enhance healthcare understanding:

- AI explanation for **medicine interactions**
- AI-based **symptom analysis**
- AI guidance for **medicine safety and precautions**
- AI reasoning for **medical emergency risks**

The platform uses **OpenAI models** to generate simplified healthcare explanations.
